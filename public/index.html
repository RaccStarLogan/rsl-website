About Me

I've always had the urge to make stuff for as long as I can remember. Heck, I've always dreamed of making my own animated show (as mentioned previously). Besides, I would always draw my own little characters, make little song doodles, and eventually I started to flesh out my characters a bit more and expand on them so that I have some material to work off of other than just designs.

By high school, I was experimenting with digital art and music more seriously. I got into remixing games like Undertale and Friday Night Funkin', which pushed me into developing my own sound and style.
