RaccStarLogan's Commission Terms of Service


1. General Terms

  • By commissioning me, you agree to these Terms of Service.
  • I reserve the right to decline any commission request for any reason.
  • These terms may be updated at any time without prior notice.
  • You must be 18 years or older to commission me, regardless of whether you're commissioning NSFW content.

2. Payment & Pricing

  • All prices are in USD.
  • Payment must be made upfront.
  • Prices may vary based on complexity, deadlines, or additional requests.

3. Process & Revisions

  • I will provide progress updates whenever possible.
  • Revisions are allowed during the sketch phase.
  • Additional revisions beyond the initial ones may incur extra fees.
  • Major changes will not be accepted once the shading process has begun (if applicable).

4. Usage & Rights

  • I retain the copyright to all artwork created.
  • Commissions are for personal use only unless otherwise agreed upon.
  • You may not claim my work as your own, resell it, or use it for commercial purposes without prior agreement.
  • I reserve the right to display commissioned work in my portfolio or on social media unless stated otherwise.
  • My artwork may not be used for NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) or AI-generation purposes.
  • You are allowed to post my work to social media (except Twitter/X)* as long as you properly credit me.

5. Commercial Use

  • Commercial usage rights must be negotiated separately.
  • Prices for commercial work are higher than for personal commissions.
  • Redistribution, resale, or use of the artwork for profit is prohibited without a proper license.

6. Deadlines & Turnaround Time

  • Estimated completion times vary based on complexity and queue.
  • If you need an expedited commission, a rush fee may apply.
  • Delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances, but I will communicate any significant changes.

7. Cancellations & Refunds

  • If I cancel a commission, a refund reflecting the progress made will be given.
  • If you cancel before work has begun, a full refund will be issued.
  • No refunds will be issued once significant progress has been made.
  • For artwork commissions, partial refunds will be issued once linework has begun. Refunds will be based on the stage of completion.
  • For voice-over or music commissions, no refunds will be issued once work has begun.

8. Content Guidelines (Will Do & Won't Do)

Will Do:

  • SFW and NSFW content
  • Original characters
  • Gore or horror themes
  • Suggestive themes

Won't Do:

  • Anything involving excretion
  • Non-consensual or illegal content
  • Hate speech or discriminatory imagery
  • Anything I find uncomfortable or inappropriate

9. Prohibited Content

  • I will not create artwork that includes hate speech, extreme violence, illegal content, or anything I find uncomfortable.
  • I reserve the right to decline commissions based on subject matter.

By commissioning me, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to these terms.


*Twitter/X uses its posts to train its generative AI. I will not be offering workarounds, so do not post my art to Twitter/X. If I find out that you have, you will be blacklisted from commissioning me until it is removed. You may post links to my art, but do not upload it directly.
